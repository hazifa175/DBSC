Scabies

caused by the Sarcoptes scabiei mite, a highly contagious skin parasite. The mites burrow into the skin and cause severe itching, which can result in the formation of scabs and hair loss. It is a nonseasonal parasite that can be found in dogs of all ages and breeds.

Care Advices

1. Medicinal baths: bath the dog regularly in chemical shampoos.
2. Give heartworm and fleas medications
3. Liquid ivermectin: This is a stronger version of the heartworm prevention medicine.
4. Keep your dog away from foxes and places where foxes go.
